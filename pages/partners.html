02

Consistent, scheduled performances that residents can look forward to, creating anticipation and routine enrichment.

03

Intergenerational Connection

Meaningful interactions between students and seniors that bridge generations and foster mutual understanding and respect.

Become a Partner Organization

Are you a senior living community, healthcare facility, or community organization interested in bringing live music to those you serve? We'd love to partner with you.

What We Look For:

  • A welcoming environment for student performers
  • A dedicated space for performances (common area, lobby, chapel, etc.)
  • An audience who would benefit from live music
  • Flexibility in scheduling performance dates and times
  • A commitment to fostering intergenerational connections
